General Responsibilities

Interns are expected to complete assigned tasks, participate in discussions, and maintain professionalism throughout the internship.

Daily Tasks

  • Check emails and messages for updates.
  • Complete assigned tasks before deadlines.
  • Attend scheduled meetings and training sessions.
  • Submit progress reports if required.

Team Collaboration

Interns should work collaboratively with their teams, seek guidance when needed, and contribute actively to discussions.

Professional Conduct

Maintain a respectful and professional attitude in all communications and interactions.
